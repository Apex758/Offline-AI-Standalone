# Trophy Image Generation Prompts

All trophies use a consistent modern 3D aesthetic. Each prompt is ready to paste into an image generator.

---

## Style Notes

- **Base style** applied to all: sleek geometric base, polished metal, physically based rendering, studio lighting, hyperrealistic textures, modern award design, isolated on pure white background, no text, no shadows, product photography style.
- **Gold tier** — `polished gold metal`
- **Silver tier** — replace with `polished silver metal, cool chrome finish`
- **Bronze tier** — replace with `polished bronze metal, warm reddish-brown finish`
